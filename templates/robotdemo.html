ROWS Robot Demo - "GSU"

For my capstone project at Georgia Southern University, my team and I were tasked to create a fleet control software for robots in the ROWS lab at the university to showcase their abilities. We designed software so that the robots would form patterns using racks to create a formation requested by the user. This is a demo of my “GSU” formation spelled out using racks. Some technologies/languages used were Java, Spring Boot, HTML, and JavaScript.
